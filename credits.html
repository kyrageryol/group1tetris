The original Tetris gameplay is known as the Soviet 'mind game' that took over the world.

A software engineer at the Soviet Academy of Sciences in Moscow created the game Tetris as a simple game to use as a test for a new computer.

The game became addicting to most as a simple, mindless puzzle.
