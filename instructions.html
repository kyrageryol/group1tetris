Instructions

After selecting the "New Game" button, you will be directed to Tetris game play.
Press SPACE to start the game.
Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the pieces as they fall, press the SPACE bar to speed up the pieces as they fall.
Use the back arrow in your browser to end the game or start over.
The objective is to arrange the pieces in an efficient way so that they do not touch the top of the screen.
