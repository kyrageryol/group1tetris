Instructions

After selecting the "New Game" button, you will be directed to a Tetris game play. There will be three modes to select from: easy, medium, & hard.

The different modes represent different speeds. Easy is the easiest and slowest speed, and hard is the fastest speed.

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the pieces as they fall, press the SPACE bar to speed up the pieces as they fall.

Use the back button to end the game or start over. The game will also reset once clicking the difficulty button.

The objective is to arrange the pieces in an efficient way so that they do not touch the top of the screen.
